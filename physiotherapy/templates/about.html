Our Story

Healing rooted in precision and care

Just as Leonardo Da Vinci studied the human body with meticulous precision to unlock its secrets, our physiotherapists apply that same spirit of curiosity, discipline, and artistry to every patient's recovery journey.

Davinci Physiotherapy was founded with a simple mission: to bring high-quality, evidence-based physiotherapy directly to patients' homes, removing the barriers of travel, waiting rooms, and scheduling stress from the path to recovery.

Our multidisciplinary team of licensed physiotherapists specializes in orthopedic, neurological, respiratory, pediatric, and geriatric rehabilitation — bringing hospital-grade care to the comfort and privacy of your own home.
